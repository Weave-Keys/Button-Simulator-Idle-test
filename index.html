Button Simulator Idle

Features

• **Endless Progression:** Unlock countless buttons, each with unique click values and upgrade paths.
• **Prestige System:** Reset your progress to gain powerful boosts, unlocking new levels of wealth and power.
• **Strategic Upgrades:** Invest wisely in upgrades to maximize your clicking efficiency and generate more coins.
• **Challenging Achievements:** Complete a variety of achievements to earn special rewards and unlock hidden content.
• **Regular Updates:** Enjoy frequent updates with new content, events, and features to keep the fun going!

How to Play

1. **Click the buttons:** Start by clicking the initial button to earn coins.
2. **Upgrade your buttons:** Use your earned coins to upgrade your buttons and increase their click values.
3. **Unlock new buttons:** As you progress, you'll unlock more powerful and valuable buttons.
4. **Prestige wisely:** Consider prestiging to gain permanent upgrades and reset your progress for exponential growth.
5. **Achieve greatness:** Complete achievements to unlock even more rewards and conquer the leaderboards!
